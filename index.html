General Guidelines

  • Warm-up: 5 min dynamic — jumping jacks, bodyweight squats, arm swings.
  • Cool-down: stretch chest, back, hamstrings, shoulders.
  • Progressive overload: add weight or reps gradually.
  • Form first: technique always beats heavier weight.
